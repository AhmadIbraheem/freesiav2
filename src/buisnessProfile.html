Who Are We

  • FREESIA is a corporate based in Turkey
  • FREESIA has both positions as a Export Management Company (EMC) and a Export Trading Company (ETC), depending on our clients' needs.
  • As An EMC, FREESIA handles export operations for a domestic company that wants to sell its product overseas
  • As an ETC, FREESIA identifies what foreign buyers want to buy and then hunts down domestic sources willing to export for that.
